Last Art - Last Dreams

Mai Ajay Mai ek artist hu mujhe drawing karna bahut pasand hai had se jyada itna ki agar mai drawing karne men lag jata hu to sab kuchh bhool ke usi men jut jata hu par dukh ki baat ye hai ki mere mata pita ko ye sab bilkul nahi pasand vo nahi chahte mai ye sab karu aur unka kahna hai ki isse meri padhal bahut disturb hoti hai. Isi liye ab mai unse chhup chhup ke tution ke jane ke bahane ek viran khali jagah pe apne passion ko follow aur decorate karne laga. Maine waha itni sketches, paintings aur art banaye ki pura diwar bhar gaya. Par mai arts ki duniya men itna kho gaya tha ki mai apne exams ko bhool hi gaya aur bina prepration ke mai exam men fail ho gaya aur result ane se kuchh din pahle hi mere pita ji ko ye bhi pata chal chuka tha ki mai tution ke bahane ghar se nikalta to tha par kabhi gaya nahi. Ye sab jankar vo already bahut gussa the mujhse baat bhi nahi karte the, ma bhi din bhar sunati rahti thi aur fir result ka wale din mai itna ghabraya hua tha ki mere andar himmat hi nahi bachchi apne ghar walo ka samna karne ka, to maine bahut himmat karke ye decision liya hai ki mai suicide karunga aur apne ghar walo ka bojh khatam kar dunga Par mai nahi chahta mere painting, arts aise hi waste ho jaye meri bas itni ichha hai ki mera art duniya ke samne aaye aur logo ko pata chale ki mai koi losser nahi bas ek unsupported and frustrated artists tha Tum jo bhi ho Agar tum yaha tak padh rahe ho to I hope tum meri madat jarur karoge Bye.
